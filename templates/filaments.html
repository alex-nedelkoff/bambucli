Filaments

Manage the colour swatches used on print labels and the in-stock list on the dashboard. Click a swatch to recolour it (or type a hex), and pick a stock status from the dropdown — changes save automatically. Add your own with the + Add tile. Names match what staff type in the colour field on a new order.

Naming convention
  • Basic: a colour name — Blue, Black, Teal.
  • Finishes: prefix with silk, dark, light, or matte — e.g. Silk Gold, Dark Teal. (Silk names also get a shiny swatch + sparkly label.)
  • Specials: metallics Silver/Gold/Copper, Glow in the Dark, Rainbow.
  • Multi-colour (2–3): combine names with /, &, +, or and — e.g. Red / Blue, White + Red + Black. The swatch blends the colours.
